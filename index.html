Description

Welcome to Fireball Heroes — A Fiery Quest! Unleash your inner flame and join a team of blazing warriors in their quest to protect their hometown from relentless alien invaders.

Train by blasting objects and perfecting your aim, then jump into battle to incinerate enemies with explosive fireballs. With exhilarating gameplay, comedic moments, and epic showdowns, this game is your ultimate ticket to action-packed fun!
